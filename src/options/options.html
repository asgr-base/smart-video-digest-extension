Smart Video Digest Settings

Automatically summarize when switching to a different YouTube tab while the side panel is open.

No shortcut assigned. Click Change to set one.

This extension uses Gemini Nano, Chrome's built-in on-device AI model.

  • All processing happens locally on your device
  • No video data is sent to external servers
  • The AI model does not learn from or store your data
  • Works offline after the initial model download
